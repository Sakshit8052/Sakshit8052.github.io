Characteristic & behaviour 🦁
Physical Traits

  • Lions have muscular body with a yellowish, brown-coat,
    powerful claws and sharp teeth.

  • Male lions weight between 150-250 kg while
    lionesses are typically 120-182 kg.

    • Social structure

  • Lions are social animals that live in prides, which are
    group of up 30 lions. The pride is composed of related females
    and their cubs and small number of adult male lions.

    • Hunting

  • Lionesses are the primary hunter, ofen working together to
    take down large prey animals like-gazelles, wildebeests, and zebras.
    Lions are powerful and can consume up to 40 kg meal in a single meal.
